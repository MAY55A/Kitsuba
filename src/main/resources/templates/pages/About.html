About Kitsuba

A small project to make learning kanji more fun

🌱 The Idea

Kitsuba is a passion project built by a solo developer who is also learning Japanese. It started as a simple way to make kanji practice more engaging and less repetitive.

Instead of feeling like a chore, the goal is to turn learning into something more visual, interactive, and enjoyable — even in small daily sessions.

🧑‍💻 Development

This website is not backed by a large team or company. It is developed and maintained by one person, which means things may not always be perfect — and that’s okay.

Kitsuba is constantly evolving, and improvements are made over time based on feedback and experimentation.

📡 Data & Accuracy

The kanji data used on Kitsuba comes from external APIs. The platform does not currently maintain its own database, though this may change in the future as the project grows.

While care is taken to provide useful and consistent information, some data may be incomplete or vary depending on the source.

⚠️ Important Note

Kitsuba should not be your only resource for learning Japanese.

It focuses mainly on kanji practice, which is only one part of the language. To learn effectively, you should also use other resources for:

  • Grammar
  • Vocabulary
  • Listening and speaking
  • Reading real-world content

Think of Kitsuba as a fun companion tool, not a complete learning solution.
