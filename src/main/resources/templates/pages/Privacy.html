Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Kitsuba is a personal project designed to help users practice kanji. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

2. Data We Collect

When using Kitsuba, the following data may be collected:

  • Email address (via Google authentication)
  • Basic profile information (name, avatar)
  • Learning progress (XP, kanji learned, stats)

3. How Data Is Used

Your data is used to:

  • Provide authentication and account access
  • Track your learning progress
  • Improve the platform experience

4. Data Storage

Kitsuba stores only the minimum data required for user accounts and progress tracking. Kanji data itself is not owned or stored permanently by the platform.

5. Data Sharing

Kitsuba does not sell or share your personal data with third parties. Authentication is handled securely through Google.
